Philippines Returns to Traditional June-to-March School Calendar Starting June 2025

The Philippines Department of Education announced that starting June 16, 2025, public schools will return to the traditional June-to-March academic calendar. This change aims to address challenges caused by extreme heat during summer months, which previously disrupted classes. The new schedule includes 197 class days and allows private schools to set their own start dates within the legal timeframe. This shift marks a significant step toward stabilizing the school year and improving learning conditions nationwide.
